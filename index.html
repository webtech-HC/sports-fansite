Gameday Guide (Unofficial)

Getting There

  • Expect road closures near campus.
  • Plan 60–90 minutes for traffic and entry.
  • Pick a meetup spot in case phones die.

Entry & Bags

  • Many venues have clear-bag rules/metal detectors.
  • Leave large items & outside alcohol at home.
  • Verify with the official event page.

Food & Water

  • Most vendors are cashless—bring a card/mobile pay.
  • Hydrate early; look for refill stations.
  • Consider ear protection for kids.

Accessibility

  • Check ADA parking, shuttles, elevators, seating.
  • Service animals typically permitted.
  • Contact accessibility office for assistance.
