Gameday Guide
Everything you need for game weekend in Knoxville—before, during, and after kickoff.
Pre-game
  • Book early, plan early. Hotels & rentals sell out. Make a Plan B in nearby towns.
  • Where to stay. Downtown & campus first; also West Knoxville, Cedar Bluff, Farragut, Alcoa/Maryville.
  • Plan B towns. Oak Ridge, Sevierville, Pigeon Forge, Lenoir City, Clinton, Maryville, Jefferson City, Dandridge, Newport.
  • Traffic. Check the live map and Waze before you move.
  • Pack list. IDs, mobile tickets, chargers, meds, water, ponchos, sunscreen, snacks. Ear protection for little ones.
Game-time
  • Hydration: refill stations at main concourses.
  • Night games can get breezy—layer up.
  • Mobile tickets: open the wallet before the scanners to avoid bottlenecks.
  • Concessions: grab at end-of-quarter to beat halftime rush.
  • Watch our Live Score Box on the home page for in-game updates.
Post-game
  • Wait 20–30 minutes if parked close—saves time overall.
  • Use side streets or river route when open to beat main flow.
  • Walk outside control points before calling rideshare.
  • 24/7 food: we’ll curate a list; submit spots on the listings page.
Emergency & Visitor Info
  • Emergency: 911 — UT Police: (865) 974-3111 — City non-emergency: (865) 215-7450.
  • First-aid stations located on main concourses; look for posted signs.
  • Accessibility: elevators & accessible routes are signed; staff can guide you.
