Gameday Guide

Everything you need for game day in Knoxville—before, during, and after kickoff.

Pre-game Game-time Post-game Families Visitors

Pre-game

Arrive early, park smart, and soak in the atmosphere around Neyland.

Fans

  • Arrive 3–4 hours early for marquee opponents; garages and lots fill fast.
  • Check our Schedule & Results for TV time changes before you leave.
  • Tailgate zones near campus fill quickly—pack in/out and keep lanes clear.

Families

  • Use restrooms/food before entering the busiest gates to avoid bottlenecks.
  • Bring ear protection for little ones; the stadium gets loud.
  • Hydration is key—sealed water bottles are typically allowed (check bag policy day-of).

Visitors / Tourists

  • First time to Neyland? Give yourself time to walk the riverfront and Vol Village.
  • Rideshare tends to surge post-game; pre-book a pickup point off major closures.
  • See the map for walking routes and campus landmarks.

Game-time

Inside the stadium: finding your seats, staying comfy, and keeping up.

All Fans

  • Mobile tickets: brighten your screen and open them before the gate.
  • Water, sunscreen, hat—day games can be hot. Night games can get breezy by the river.
  • Keep an eye on our Live Score Box on the home page—updates post as we receive them.

Accessibility

  • Use elevators and accessible seating areas as posted; ask any event staff for directions.
  • Service animals permitted; please follow venue guidance.

Post-game

Leaving smart and spots to wind down nearby.

Traffic Tips

  • Wait 20–30 minutes if you parked close—let the first wave clear.
  • Use the traffic map (right rail) to spot closures and heavy flow.
  • Rideshare: walk a few blocks away from hard closures before requesting.

Food & Drink

  • Check Specials for fan-friendly spots and hours.
  • Family sections and quieter eats are a short drive north of campus.
