Gameday Guide

Pre-game Game-time Post-game

Pre-game

Book early, plan early. Knoxville and nearby cities sell out fast on home weekends. Always make a Plan B in surrounding towns.

Where to stay

Hotels & Motels

  • Downtown & campus hotels fill first; expand search to West Knoxville, Oak Ridge, Farragut, Sevierville, Alcoa/Maryville.
  • Expect 2-night minimums and higher rates for big opponents.

Vacation rentals

  • Airbnb/VRBO go fast—book early and verify parking rules.

Campgrounds / RV

  • Look outside Knoxville for availability; confirm hookups and game-day access windows.

Plan B towns

Oak Ridge • Sevierville • Pigeon Forge • Gatlinburg • Lenoir City • Loudon • Clinton • Morristown • Jefferson City • Dandridge • Newport • Greeneville • Bristol (tri-cities)

Getting here & parking
  • Arrive on campus 3–4 hours pre-kick for big games.
  • Use the live traffic and “mini map” in the right rail to pick faster routes.
  • Rideshare: agree on pickup point away from closures before the game.
Pack list (go-bag)
  • Mobile tickets (screen brightness up)
  • Clear bag or small clutch
  • Refillable water bottle (if allowed)
  • Portable phone charger
  • Rain layer / poncho
  • Hat / sunscreen
  • Ear protection for kids
  • Cashless payment ready
  • Printed directions as backup
  • Small first-aid kit & meds
With kids
  • Family restrooms and changing tables available—ask nearest staff.
  • Pack wipes and noise protection; halftime can be busy.

Game-time

  • Hydration: refill stations at main concourses.
  • Night games get breezy—bring a layer.
  • Mobile tickets: open before scanners to avoid bottlenecks.
  • Concessions: grab at end of quarter to beat lines.
Check our Live Score Box on the home page for in-game updates.

Post-game

  • Wait 20–30 minutes if parked close—saves time overall.
  • Use side streets or river route when open to beat main flow.
  • Walk outside control points before calling rideshare.
